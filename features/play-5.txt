Feature: Play-5

Play-5 is a single-player team-game mode.
One must be in a team in order to play this game mode.

The game requires a selection of players, of which:
- 1 is the player
- 1 may be a GK Bot
- 3 or 4 are customly selected with priority:
-- 1- teammates
-- 2- mates
-- 2- teamless
-- 3- others

The result of the game is based on the balance of the combined generic attributes of the players.

The field-players will be sorted into 4 positions, in a determinable formation (2-2, 1-2-1, 2-1-1, 1-1-2)

Some attributes will enhance the result of the team.
The better the attributes for a position, the better the contribution for the team.
Positions are:

LF CF RF 2 2 2
LW CM RW 1 2 1
LB CB RB 2 2 2

the further forwards, the more atk is relevant.
the further backwards, the more def is relevant.
