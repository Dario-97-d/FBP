-- FBP replanning --

- Main features:
Play-3
Play-5
Play-7
Play-11

Play-3:
- pvp in a 3v3 match (1 bot + 2p vs 3 bot)
- performance based on comparison with chosen opponent

Play-5:
- player must be in a team
- team plays with bots if it hasn't enough players
- team plays against other teams
- team plays against bot team if no teams available
- play-5 player ranking based on real (no-bot) wins

Play-7:
- team can have permanent bots
- team formation and player positions
- team can enter competitions (1 match per 30/60 min)
- player-team commitment per day

Play-11:
- club plays in a competition
- divisions system, with new clubs joining the lowest divisions
- permanent calendar (1 real day -> 1 matchweek)
- team formation, player positions, tactics
- player-team commitment per season
